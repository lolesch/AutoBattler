### Definition

**Chaining:** After a weapon. Multiple Amplifiers chain freely behind a weapon

> [!tldr]+ Description
> Adds bonuses to the nearest upstream weapon's output stats.

> [!quote]- Purpose - *Why is this essential?*
> Stat scaling besides [[Weapon]] upgrades

> [!check]- Reward - *What is the gain?*
> Higher stats with no conditions attached.

> [!warning]- Risk - *What are the punishments*
> Pure inventory cost - slots cannot hold items with build-defining effects.

> [!fail]- Opposition - *What counters this?*
> Limited inventory space

> [!error]- Polarity - *What increases its weakness?*
> Big item sizes.

> [!example]- Progress - *What is the goal*
> Build optimization and power spikes.
> Stack until space cost exceeds gain;

> [!info]- Depth - *Where are the synergies*
> Should also work with [[Payload]] stats <- review this

> [!tip]- Appeal - *Does it help the game*
> Immediate, readable feedback loop.
